## The Universal Control Pattern

Every social system has a hierarchical control structure with the same basic shape:

1. **Supreme Authority** — issues doctrine, law, strategy, or policy
2. **Intermediate Controllers** — interpret and transmit to local level
3. **Local Community** — enforces norms through social mechanisms
4. **Individual** — internalizes norms and self-regulates

The key structural questions STPA asks of each system:

- How rich is the **feedback** from bottom to top?
- How **self-sealing** are the process models at each level?
- Where are the **accountability voids** — places where the controller and the interested party are the same entity?
- What **circuit breakers** exist to prevent escalation?
