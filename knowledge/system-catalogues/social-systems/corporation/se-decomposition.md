# Corporation (For-Profit) — SE Decomposition

Extracted from `projects/systems-introduction-book/docs/part2/ten-systems.md`
§2.2.1. IDs use the `C*` prefix as in the source.

## Goals (CG)

- **CG1** — Generate sustainable profit and shareholder value
- **CG2** — Deliver products or services that satisfy market demand
- **CG3** — Provide livelihood and purpose to employees
- **CG4** — Ensure organizational continuity beyond any individual

## Requirements (CR)

- **CR1** — Legal incorporation with limited liability
- **CR2** — Governance structure — board of directors accountable to shareholders
- **CR3** — Revenue generation exceeding costs (business model)
- **CR4** — Hierarchical management with clear authority and accountability
- **CR5** — Employment contracts defining mutual obligations
- **CR6** — Regulatory compliance — tax, labor, environmental, industry-specific

## Functions (CF)

- **CF1** — Strategic direction — set vision, approve budgets, appoint executives
- **CF2** — Product/service delivery — design, produce, sell, support
- **CF3** — Financial management — accounting, treasury, investor relations
- **CF4** — Human resource management — recruit, develop, compensate, terminate
- **CF5** — Legal and compliance — contracts, IP protection, regulatory filings
- **CF6** — External relations — marketing, PR, government affairs

## Logical Architecture (CL)

- **CL1** — Governance Subsystem — board, shareholders' meeting, audit committee
- **CL2** — Operations Subsystem — product development, manufacturing, supply chain
- **CL3** — Financial Subsystem — accounting, treasury, controlling
- **CL4** — HR Subsystem — recruiting, payroll, training, labor relations
- **CL5** — Legal & Compliance Subsystem — general counsel, regulatory, IP

## Physical Implementation (CP)

- **CP1** — Articles of incorporation, shareholder register, board resolutions
- **CP2** — CEO, C-suite, management hierarchy, org chart
- **CP3** — Factories, offices, IT infrastructure, supply chain partners
- **CP4** — ERP system (SAP, Oracle), bank accounts, annual report
- **CP5** — Employment contracts, HR software (Workday), training programs
