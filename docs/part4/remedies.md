## Five Architectural Principles for Safer Social Systems

### 1. Close the Feedback Loop
Establish independent feedback channels that bypass the authority hierarchy. Require periodic measurement of actual wellbeing, not just compliance.

### 2. Make Process Models Updatable
Institute version control for doctrine and policy. Require engagement with external knowledge. Preserve dissenting opinions.

### 3. Separate Control Functions
Separate spiritual/ideological authority from administrative authority. Separate care from enforcement. Establish independent safeguarding.

### 4. Install Circuit Breakers
Define escalation limits on in-group/out-group rhetoric. Require proportionality review for sanctions. Ensure informed consent for intensive practices.

### 5. Protect Vulnerable Populations
Apply external safeguarding standards to all settings involving children. Prohibit fear-based instruction for minors. Require mandatory external reporting of abuse.
