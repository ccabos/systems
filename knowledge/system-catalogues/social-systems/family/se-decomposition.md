# Family — SE Decomposition

Extracted from `projects/systems-introduction-book/docs/part2/ten-systems.md`
§2.3.1. IDs use the `F*` prefix as in the source.

## Goals (FG)

- **FG1** — Ensure biological reproduction and survival of offspring
- **FG2** — Provide emotional security, love, and belonging
- **FG3** — Socialize children into cultural norms and competences
- **FG4** — Pool resources and manage economic risk collectively
- **FG5** — Transmit identity, heritage, and property across generations

## Requirements (FR)

- **FR1** — Pair bond or co-parenting agreement (marriage, partnership, co-habitation)
- **FR2** — Shelter and material provision for dependents
- **FR3** — Emotional availability and care between members
- **FR4** — Authority structure for child-rearing decisions
- **FR5** — Inheritance and succession norms (legal or customary)
- **FR6** — Interface with external systems — schools, healthcare, state

## Functions (FF)

- **FF1** — Nurture — feed, shelter, protect, and care for dependents
- **FF2** — Socialize — teach language, norms, values, and skills
- **FF3** — Govern — make decisions on discipline, education, health, finances
- **FF4** — Economic management — earn, budget, save, invest for the household
- **FF5** — Ritual and identity maintenance — holidays, traditions, storytelling
- **FF6** — External representation — interface with school, state, community

## Logical Architecture (FL)

- **FL1** — Parental Authority Subsystem — decision-making, discipline, role modeling
- **FL2** — Care Subsystem — nurturing, health management, emotional support
- **FL3** — Economic Subsystem — income, budgeting, property management
- **FL4** — Socialization Subsystem — education support, cultural transmission, values

## Physical Implementation (FP)

- **FP1** — Home — house or apartment, shared living space
- **FP2** — Parents/guardians, their partnership agreement (marriage certificate, etc.)
- **FP3** — Family income, bank accounts, property, inheritance documents
- **FP4** — Daily routines, family rituals, photo albums, heirlooms
- **FP5** — School enrollment, pediatrician, community/religious membership
