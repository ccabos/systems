# University — SE Decomposition

Extracted from `projects/systems-introduction-book/docs/part2/ten-systems.md`
§2.2.2. IDs use the `U*` prefix as in the source.

## Goals (UG)

- **UG1** — Advance human knowledge through research
- **UG2** — Educate and credential the next generation of professionals and scholars
- **UG3** — Serve society through knowledge transfer and public engagement
- **UG4** — Preserve academic freedom and institutional autonomy

## Requirements (UR)

- **UR1** — Peer-reviewed research as the standard for knowledge validation
- **UR2** — Structured curricula leading to recognized degrees
- **UR3** — Academic self-governance — faculty senates, elected rectors/deans
- **UR4** — Sustainable funding — tuition, grants, endowments, state funding
- **UR5** — Tenure system protecting freedom of inquiry
- **UR6** — Accreditation by external quality bodies

## Functions (UF)

- **UF1** — Research — conduct and publish original scholarship
- **UF2** — Teaching — deliver lectures, seminars, labs, examinations
- **UF3** — Academic governance — senate decisions, faculty appointments, curriculum design
- **UF4** — Student administration — admissions, enrollment, grading, graduation
- **UF5** — Financial management — budgets, grant administration, tuition collection
- **UF6** — Knowledge transfer — patents, spin-offs, public lectures, policy advice

## Logical Architecture (UL)

- **UL1** — Academic Governance Subsystem — senate, rector, faculty councils
- **UL2** — Research Subsystem — departments, institutes, labs, libraries
- **UL3** — Teaching Subsystem — curricula, lecture halls, examination offices
- **UL4** — Administration Subsystem — student office, finance, HR, facilities

## Physical Implementation (UP)

- **UP1** — University charter, Grundordnung, accreditation certificates
- **UP2** — Rector/President, deans, senate, faculty councils
- **UP3** — Laboratories, libraries, lecture halls, research institutes
- **UP4** — Student registry (HISinOne, Campus Management), examination office
- **UP5** — Endowment, state funding agreements, DFG/ERC grants
