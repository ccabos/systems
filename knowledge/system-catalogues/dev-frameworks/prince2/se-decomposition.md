# PRINCE2 — SE Decomposition

Extracted from `projects/systems-introduction-book/docs/part3/frameworks.md`
§2.3. IDs use the `P*` prefix as in the source.

## Goals (PG)

- **PG1** — Deliver projects within controlled parameters of time, cost, quality, scope, risk, and benefits
- **PG2** — Ensure continued business justification throughout the project
- **PG3** — Provide clear governance, accountability, and decision-making authority

## Requirements (PR)

- **PR1** — Business Case justifying the project, reviewed at every stage boundary
- **PR2** — Defined organization structure — Project Board, Project Manager, Team Manager
- **PR3** — Management by stages — authorization given one stage at a time
- **PR4** — Management by exception — tolerances defined; escalation when breached
- **PR5** — Product-based planning — focus on deliverables, not tasks
- **PR6** — Lessons learned captured and applied

## Functions (PF)

- **PF1** — Directing — Project Board authorizes stages, makes go/no-go decisions
- **PF2** — Managing — Project Manager plans, monitors, controls, and reports
- **PF3** — Delivering — Team Managers create products per Work Packages
- **PF4** — Business justification — maintain and review Business Case
- **PF5** — Risk management — identify, assess, mitigate, escalate risks
- **PF6** — Quality management — define quality criteria, review products
- **PF7** — Change control — assess and authorize changes to baseline

## Logical Architecture (PL)

- **PL1** — Direction Subsystem — Project Board, stage-gate authorization
- **PL2** — Management Subsystem — Project Manager, plans, controls, reports
- **PL3** — Delivery Subsystem — Team Managers, Work Packages, product creation
- **PL4** — Business Justification Subsystem — Business Case, benefits review
- **PL5** — Assurance Subsystem — quality review, risk register, change authority

## Physical Implementation (PP)

- **PP1** — Project Initiation Document (PID), Business Case
- **PP2** — Stage Plan, Product Breakdown Structure, Work Packages
- **PP3** — Highlight Reports, End Stage Reports, Lessons Log
- **PP4** — Risk Register, Issue Register, Change Authority records
- **PP5** — Quality Register, product descriptions with quality criteria
